WHAT DO WE TEACH ?

To help learners survive the storms of job hunting, we teach skills that fall in three broad categories, namely; goal setting and personal branding, professional writing, and job hunting and networking skills. These skills cover everything our learners need to pick a career path, and develop the character traits needed to thrive in job hunting.

HOW DO WE TEACH ?

Learning the skills for job hunting is far different from learning hard skills and thus we teach it with emotion. Intentionally, we create friendly classrooms where students can freely express themselves and feel safe enough to discuss and challenge their vulnerabilities, all while leaning forward to unlock hidden potential though engaging hands-on learning.

HOW DO YOU LEARN ?

We work with leading employers across the world to design case studies that mimic real world recruitment processes which the learners take. This allows the learners on our programs to see first hand the challenges they could face and ask for our help before mistakes are consequential. We foster creativity and originality by allowing our learning to work independently and provide actionable feedback on every minute detail of their output.
