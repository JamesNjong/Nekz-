Campus training and workshops

We design and teach short courses on professional development, to school and small groups. Our trainings facilitate the aquisition of the soft skills needed to thrive both on the job and in job hunting.

Our workshops and short courses cover a range of skills such as; personal goal setting, building a personal brand, networking, writing professional e-mails and crafting noticeable CVs and cover letters.
