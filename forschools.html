We love working with schools, and we make them profitable and easy to manage.

We work with schools to manage the expectations of students and parents, by designing feedback mechanisms that allow school managers to make informed decisions about their program delivery and take advantage of opportunities lurking in the minds of parents.

Schools so often find it hard to say with certainty what the intake for the new year will be and thus cannot budget or plan ahead often because parents randomly move students around.

No school owner wants to have empty classrooms. And traditional advertising is not the way to because parents choose schools based on recommendations and the experience of other students.

Our work allows for school managers to get ahead of this problem by using advanced data analytics to identify expectations and avenues where the students need support, and get ahead of problems long before anyone notices they exist.

We use a mix of advanced statistic and machine learning tools to dig into the facts in collected data, and leverage our own experience and knowledge to highlight the relevant action items for the managers. All we do is make decision making easy, and push the boundaries of what an optimal school experience should be.
