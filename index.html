We are storytellers better than most, and we are not entrepreneurs.

At Nekz, we pride ourselves with helping job seekers find the right words to tell compelling narratives about who they are, what they know, and why they are the best candidates for the job.

We teach people how to use stories in job hunting.

For everyone who has mastered a skill, getting a job comes down to convincing an employer your heart pumps blood, and its red.
Employers want to know who you are, and why you are the best candidates; and stories are the best way to help them see you.

We help people master how to tell stories that highlight the best things about them.

We tell stories that open doors for people.

Job hunting can be hard for some people, and finding the words to put on their cover letters or CV’s can take forever. Sometimes for them, its just plane hard to manage an interview.

We take this away, helping them write these letters to get their foot in the door, and simulating interviews until they are ready to face them.

We don’t assume know, we teach them to be sure.

School teaches you what to do at job but almost never teaches you how to get a job. Everyone assumes it is easy to find a job until you start searching.

We take that assumption away, designing and teaching job readiness programs in schools and short workshops for groups of early career professionals.
