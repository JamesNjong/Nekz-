We are the storytellers, helping job seekers and entrepreneurs, access opportunities.

At dropstori, we help job seekers and entrepreneurs find the right words to tell compelling narratives about who they are, what they know, and why they are the best options for the opportunities they seek.

OUR SERVICES CONTACT US

We use storytelling to help people get jobs.

For everyone who has mastered a skill, getting a job comes down to convincing an employer your heart pumps blood, and its red.
Employers want to know who you are, and why you are the best candidate; and stories are the best way to help them see, and trust you.

We help people master how to tell stories that highlight the best things about them.

We help entrepreneurs raise funds, using storytelling.

It is not enough to have a great product or idea. Building a thriving business requires an ability to communicate the value of this idea or the benefits of the product, and often entrepreneurs struggle enormously with this.

We take this stress away, helping them write compelling pitch decks and prepare presentations that use storytelling to capture the essence of their ideas and products.

We make schools better and worthwhile.

For every school owner or manager, questions like whats your estimated intake for the next year, whats your budget or what new activities are you planning for the students to improve their outcomes are generally hard and most often not considered.

Using advanced analytics and tiny bits of mathematical optimization, we help schools better plan, and better serve their students and the parents who choose them. We help them differentiate with action driven value propositions that improve enrollment numbers and student outcomes.
